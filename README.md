# Recent Research Progress on AI-Based Building Energy Prediction and Operation

This repository hosts a public webpage listing selected recent papers on AI-based building energy prediction and operation.

## Website

https://ssl3215.github.io/ai-building-energy-prediction/

## Scope

This webpage presents selected recent papers related to AI-based building energy prediction and operation, including building energy consumption prediction, energy disaggregation, HVAC operation, occupancy and air-conditioning behavior prediction, microclimate-informed building simulation, physics-informed modeling, smart building energy management, and uncertainty quantification of building design loads.

## Keywords

building energy prediction; building energy operation; artificial intelligence; machine learning; energy consumption; building simulation; building energy consumption prediction; energy disaggregation; HVAC energy management; physics-informed building energy modeling.

## Note

This webpage is a selected paper list, not a systematic review.
